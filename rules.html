rules of polyNexus

We want polyNexus to be a safe haven for ideas and connections to flourish.
As such, we need to establish a code of conduct to be abided to by all of
our users on our site. Make sure you read carefully and keep them in mind
so we can achieve the polyNexus dream!

Each user may only create and use ONE account at any point of time. Making multiple accounts or sharing accounts with other users is not allowed.


Remember to be nice. Harassment or other forms of antagonism is strictly forbidden the polyNexus community. Users who find others of being antagonistic can block them freely.


Excessive vulgarity is not welcome. Mild cursing or profanities are allowed to be used, but not if they begin to violate rules #2 and #3.


We are a PG13 community. 18+/NSFW content such as drug use or overtly sexual topics are not allowed.


Do not post personally identifiable information (i.e. name, address, email, phone number, etc.)


Do not post copyrighted material such as magazine scans


Do not spam or flood the blogs. Keep your blog contributions to one post at a time.


Keep your blog posts relevant to the topic.


Personal attacks are not allowed. Criticism of all kinds is welcome and encouraged, but attacking other users personally is strictly forbidden.


Linking to malware/adware/spyware/software of spurious intent or description is not allowed.


Advertisements and referral links are explicitly forbidden.


Autoloading content is not allowed.


Publicly post or otherwise share PMs with other users unless you have the expressed permission of the sender to do so.

Posting copyrighted material such as magazine scans


Do not spam in message groups or in private messages.


Linking to malware/adware/spyware/software with malicious intent or description is not allowed.


Advertisements and referral links are explicitly forbidden.
