Rules of polyNexus

We want polyNexus to be a safe haven for ideas and connections to flourish. As such, we need to establish a code of conduct to be abided to by all of our users on our site. Make sure you read carefully and keep them in mind so we can achieve the polyNexus dream!

Each user may only create and use ONE account at any point of time. Making multiple accounts or sharing accounts with other users is not allowed.

Remember to be nice. Harassment or other forms of antagonism is strictly forbidden the polyNexus community. Users who find others of being antagonistic can block them freely.

Excessive vulgarity is not welcome. Mild cursing or profanities are allowed to be used, but not if they begin to violate rules #2 and #3.

We are a PG13 community. 18+/NSFW content such as drug use or overly sexual topics are not allowed.

We are also meant to be a universally open community where everyone should feel safe. Openly discussing controversial topics such as religion is not advised.

Do not post your or other people's personal information (i.e. name, address, email, phone number, etc.) Sending your own information over the private message platform is allowed but we advise you to do so with caution.

Do not post copyrighted material or anyone else's work such as images or videos

Do not spam or flood the blogs. Keep your blog contributions to one post at a time.

Keep your blog posts relevant to the topic.

Personal attacks are not allowed. Criticism of all kinds is welcome and encouraged, but attacking other users personally is strictly forbidden.

Linking to malware/adware/spyware/software of malicious intent is not allowed. This includes shock sites. A shock site is a website that is intended to be offensive, disgusting, or disturbing to its viewers.

Advertisements and referral links are explicitly forbidden.

Autoloading content is not allowed.

Publicly post or otherwise share PMs with other users unless you have the expressed permission of the sender to do so. This includes messages on other platforms, as private messages are meant to be private and not shared.

Do not spam in message groups or in private messages.

Private messages are meant to be private between sender and recipient, hence the content of such messages is unmonitored. You may send referral links and such to friends if appropriate, but sending these to strangers may get you blocked by them.

Linking to malware/adware/spyware/software of malicious intent is not allowed. This includes shock sites. A shock site is a website that is intended to be offensive, disgusting, or disturbing to its viewers.

Advertisements and referral links are explicitly forbidden.

Do not spam the event planner with entries

Do not create misleading events or mislabel an event. This includes announcing events that you have no intention to host or do not exist.
