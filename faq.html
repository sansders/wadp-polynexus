How do I create an account? Sign up on our home page with your NYP email and set a password

Is there a way for users to be banned? No, but users can block anyone they feel like. polyNexus accounts are also linked to school emails, which can be used to identify repeat offenders.

Can I change my username? Yes, just go to the settings tab.
How do I start a chat? Start a chat by simply typing in your interest you would like to discuss. e.g; Football, League of Legends

What is the chat labelling standard? Use appropriate spacings and do not use abbrevations. e.g; LOL as an exclamation and LoL the game are very different.

Is there any way to send private messages? Use the private messaging option instead of the group chat one.
Is there any rules or guidelines for the blogs? Yes, go check out our rules page

How are blogs generated? Blogs are generated for each chat group
